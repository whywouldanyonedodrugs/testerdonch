# Profitability-First Family Redevelopment Policy

## Decision

Research rules exist to make profitability claims trustworthy, not to prevent the search for profitable definitions.

The project distinguishes:

```text
translation:
    one exact feature, threshold, direction, context, execution and horizon contract

family:
    the broader causal mechanism and compelled-actor hypothesis
```

A failed translation remains failed. A family may remain open for disciplined redevelopment.

## Family redevelopment is allowed

A previously tested family may be redeveloped when one or more of these conditions apply:

- the first translation was hand-written and underidentified;
- new source data or better semantics become available;
- raw-magnitude analysis shows the standardized rule did not represent the intended mechanism;
- development-fold response surfaces indicate a materially different sign, shape, horizon, context or interaction;
- a technical or timestamp repair changes the observable decision state;
- a new pre-decision context creates a genuinely narrower conditional sleeve;
- a cross-family synthesis identifies a different compelled actor or balance-sheet transition.

Every redevelopment receives a new translation ID, inherits the full prior attempt lineage and multiplicity, and records why it is materially different.

## What closure means

```text
translation_rejected:
    do not present that exact rule as profitable;
    do not rerun cosmetic nearby variants as independent evidence

family_exhausted:
    no credible mechanism-preserving redevelopment path remains

family_development_active:
    further registered development is justified
```

A family is not exhausted merely because one or several guessed definitions were negative.

## Search freedom and evidence limits

Development may explore:

- direction;
- raw and normalized feature forms;
- continuous response shape;
- thresholds;
- horizons;
- causal contexts;
- component interactions;
- execution modes;
- payoff archetypes;
- conditional and narrow sleeves.

The search must occur inside a declared development protocol. All explored cells count toward multiplicity.

A later untouched fold cannot be used to rewrite the rule that produced it. When the full historical period has already been broadly inspected by the programme, results are labelled `program_exposed_development_evidence`; they may guide future rules but are not independent validation.

## Profitability objective

Candidate selection should optimize the economic objective appropriate to the small account, not generic statistical uniformity.

Relevant dimensions include:

```text
net expectancy
expected log-growth under capped risk
opportunity frequency
capital occupancy
left-tail and drawdown
losing-streak survivability
execution margin over costs
capacity at intended notional
context overlap with other sleeves
complexity and multiplicity burden
```

Low capacity, sparse opportunity, directional asymmetry and context dependence are acceptable when they are observable before entry and honestly bounded.

## Gates

Hard gates remain limited to integrity, authority, causality, reproducibility, protected-data safety and exact execution identity.

Economic, concentration, median, bootstrap and stress results classify or route candidates. They do not universally terminate all further family research.

A candidate may continue to controls or prospective evidence when it has meaningful positive net expectancy but is:

- uncertain;
- context-dependent;
- time- or symbol-concentrated;
- convex;
- execution-sensitive;
- sample-limited.

## Prohibited conduct

This policy does not permit:

- hidden variants;
- best-year or best-symbol rules selected after outcomes;
- retroactive pass labels;
- calling development results validation;
- using protected data for selection;
- ignoring costs or execution;
- redefining a family without new lineage and multiplicity;
- combining weak correlated variants to manufacture profitability.
