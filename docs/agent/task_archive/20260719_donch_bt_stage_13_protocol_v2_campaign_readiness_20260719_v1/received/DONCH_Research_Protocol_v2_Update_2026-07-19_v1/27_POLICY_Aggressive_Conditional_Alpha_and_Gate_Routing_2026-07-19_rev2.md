# Aggressive Conditional-Alpha and Gate-Routing Policy, rev2

The programme is aggressive in search breadth and opportunity capture while preserving hard integrity and protected-data rules.

New clarifications:

1. A rejected translation does not automatically close the broader family.
2. Registered family redevelopment may search direction, feature form, threshold, horizon, context and interactions inside development folds.
3. Development search is legitimate when all cells are registered and later evaluation blocks do not feed backward.
4. Year, symbol, market-day, median, bootstrap and stress results route claims according to payoff archetype; they are not universal family-kill gates.
5. A candidate beam of several diverse definitions is preferable to one guessed rule or hundreds of correlated variants.
6. One exact campaign approval may authorize multiple named hypotheses and phases under a frozen manifest.
7. Program-exposed historical results remain developmental and require later independent or prospective evidence.

Hard gates remain source authority, causality, protected exclusion, exact execution identity, deterministic replay, complete attempt registration and honest claims.

Profitability is the research objective. Statistical rules determine how confidently and narrowly profitability may be claimed; they do not exist to suppress disciplined search.
