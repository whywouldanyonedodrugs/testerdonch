# Test and Evidence Standards, rev5

## Separation of activities

```text
measurement
registered development search
translation freeze
outer evaluation
rolling replication
controls
independent validation
deployment
```

Development is allowed to search. Confirmation and validation require later evidence.

## Family and translation

A failed exact translation is immutable. The parent family may be redeveloped under `FAMILY_REDEFINITION_POLICY` with new IDs, full prior multiplicity and an explicit independent-evidence plan.

## Search registration

Every feature, transformation, threshold, model, rule, direction, horizon and context explored in outcome-bearing development is an attempt. Store its fold, data hashes, results and disposition.

## Economic run admission

Before development or outer evaluation, bind:

- mechanism and actor;
- measurement semantics and raw magnitude;
- event frequency;
- search space and budget;
- fold schedule and exposure class;
- candidate-selection algorithm;
- payoff archetype;
- costs and execution;
- controls;
- multiplicity and route rules.

## Gates

Hard gates: authority, causal availability, protected safety, deterministic identity, execution identity and reproducibility.

Economic and generality diagnostics route candidates. The exact contract may require positive mean, median or other utility according to archetype. Concentration is not automatically bad when the strategy is explicitly narrow or conditional.

## Multiple testing

Use nested/rolling evaluation, a bounded candidate beam and dependence-aware family correction. Report effective trials and prior related attempts. A favourable development surface is not confirmation.

## Campaign approval

One exact campaign-level approval may cover several named hypotheses and outcome-bearing phases when every source, search space, fold, selection rule, cost, stop condition and review requirement is frozen.

## Validation

Historical programme-exposed folds can support development and internal replication. Validation requires prospective or otherwise independent evidence. Protected data remain unavailable for discovery unless their purpose is explicitly changed by a separate human decision.
