# QLMG Next Research Plan

## 1. Decision

The next research program should not begin with another broad grid or another renamed chart family.

The first wave should study three connected but separable mechanisms:

1. **De-betaed residual-shock path bifurcation**
2. **Spot-led versus perp-led impulse**
3. **Point-in-time cohort breadth and dispersion**

The first is the best immediate directional mechanism. The second is the cleanest new distinction between persistent demand and leveraged local pressure. The third is a context mechanism rather than a standalone trading strategy, but it has unusually high information value because it can explain why continuation, failure, catalyst, and residual-shock families work or fail.

No economic run is authorized by this plan. The plan first requires outcome-free authority, data, universe, and generator gates. Each eventual economic run requires a separately frozen contract and explicit approval.

## C01 — De-betaed residual-shock path bifurcation

**Mechanism and compelled actor.** A crypto-perpetual move that remains after removing causal BTC/ETH exposure may reflect asset-specific information or demand. A smooth move suggests persistent distributed participation; a move concentrated in one or two bars suggests urgent flow, a one-off catalyst, or temporary liquidity withdrawal. The proposed sign is conditional:

```text
smooth residual shock -> continuation question
jump-dominated residual shock -> failure/reversal question
```

**Provenance and nearest prior.** Deep Research `DRK_M03` and `DRK_M04`; public-data mechanism map; context-and-interaction map. Closest existing work is A1/A4 continuation, relative-strength breakout, BTC-led diffusion H43, and completed-failure families.

**Distinctness.** It is distinct only if the tested component is:

```text
causal de-betaing
× path composition
```

It is not distinct if it becomes another raw breakout, relative-strength cutoff, large-candle fade, or BTC-laggard screen.

**Repetition guard.** Do not reuse the best A1, H43, prior-high, relative-strength, RFBS, or Backside thresholds. Do not allow residualization or the smooth/jump metric to become an unrestricted feature search.

**Data and protected boundary.** Current 5-minute Kraken trade and mark bars can support a bounded 2023-2025 test. Cross-sectional interpretation requires either a repaired point-in-time universe or a narrowly predeclared continuously eligible anchor cohort. No 2026 data are required. July capture may later calibrate strategy-agnostic execution costs only.

**Controls and ablations.**

- raw return without de-betaing;
- de-betaed residual without path classification;
- path classification without residualization;
- realized volatility;
- largest-bar share or jump contribution;
- matched symbol/time/parent-volatility events;
- BTC-only versus BTC+ETH residualization;
- continuation and reversal branches reported separately.

**Main falsification.** Reject the distinct mechanism if residualization and path composition add no stable post-cost information beyond raw return, volatility, and parent-market controls.

**Research economics.**

```text
information value: very high
implementation cost: moderate
overlap: moderate, but controllable through explicit ablations
```

**Disposition:** `historically_testable_after_eligibility_gate`; first-wave rank 1.

## C02 — Spot-led versus perp-led impulse

**Mechanism and compelled actor.** Persistent spot buying or selling changes underlying inventory and should be harder to reverse than a move led mainly by leveraged perpetual positioning. Perp-led moves may continue while leverage still produces price progress, but become vulnerable after completed failure.

**Provenance and nearest prior.** Forum hypothesis H02; external/forum synthesis; Kraken-native mechanism research. Closest existing work is BTC-led alt diffusion H43, relative-strength breakout, parent impulse context, and price-progress crowding ideas.

**Distinctness.** This is same-asset market leadership, not cross-coin lag selection and not a hard ALT/BTC breakout gate.

**Repetition guard.** Do not buy laggards after a BTC impulse. Do not choose lead/lag windows by the most profitable result. Do not infer spot leadership from mark-minus-last or another venue.

**Data and protected boundary.** Historical Kraken spot/reference data have not been established as acquired. An outcome-free public Kraken spot acquisition and synchronized symbol map are prerequisites. Execution remains in Kraken perpetuals only. No protected capture outcome is needed.

**Controls and ablations.**

- synchronized impulse without leadership classification;
- spot-only return;
- perp-only return;
- simultaneous impulse;
- shuffled leadership labels;
- beta- and volatility-matched events;
- breadth confirmation removed;
- spot-led continuation versus perp-led completed-failure branches.

**Main falsification.** Reject if spot-led and perp-led states do not differ after timestamp synchronization, parent beta, volatility, costs, and completed-failure controls.

**Research economics.**

```text
information value: very high
implementation cost: medium-high because new data must be acquired and synchronized
overlap: low-to-moderate after the H43 laggard branch is explicitly excluded
```

**Disposition:** `infrastructure_first_then_historically_testable`; first-wave rank 2.

# 7. Proposed design boundaries for the shortlisted mechanisms

## 7.1 C01 design boundary

### Primary question

Does the combination of causal residualization and path composition determine whether an idiosyncratic Kraken perp shock continues or reverses?

### Minimal architecture

```text
universe:
    PIT-clean universe, or a narrow continuously eligible anchor cohort

state:
    causal BTC/ETH residual return
    one primary path-efficiency measure
    one robustness path measure
    parent-market and realized-volatility context

branches:
    positive smooth
    positive jump-dominated
    negative smooth
    negative jump-dominated

entry:
    close-confirmed / next executable bar
    no touch fills

exit:
    one simple fixed-horizon family plus one structure-state end
    no partials or adds in first proof

cost:
    conservative all-taker base
```

### Parameter discipline

- one primary residual model and at most one robustness specification;
- one primary path measure and at most one robustness measure;
- bounded, predeclared quantile/state definitions rather than a dense grid;
- long and short branches registered separately;
- no rank selection from post-cost results.

### Decisive outcome

The question is answered if the residual/path components either:

1. add stable value over raw-return/volatility controls; or
2. fail cleanly and can be closed without another nearby path search.

## 7.2 C02 design boundary

### Primary question

Do spot-led impulses persist more reliably than perp-led impulses, and do perp-led moves become vulnerable only after completed failure?

### Minimal architecture

```text
input:
    synchronized public Kraken spot/reference and perp trade/mark bars

leadership:
    predeclared signed lead-lag state
    no other-venue execution or portability assumption

branches:
    spot-led continuation
    simultaneous impulse
    perp-led continuation
    perp-led completed failure

execution:
    Kraken perpetual only
```

### Parameter discipline

- synchronize clocks and bar boundaries before any return comparison;
- use a small fixed set of economically meaningful lead windows;
- separate BTC/ETH from alts;
- do not introduce OI or funding until the base price-leadership mechanism is tested;
- do not convert the test into cross-coin laggard selection.

### Decisive outcome

Reject the mechanism if leadership classification adds no stable information after parent beta, volatility, breadth, costs, and completed-failure controls.

## 7.3 C03 design boundary

### Primary question

Does contemporaneous cohort participation and dispersion add stable incremental information to a frozen price mechanism?

### Minimal architecture

```text

    PIT Kraken contract state first

cohorts:
    listing age

    fixed major/liquid-alt grouping
    stable functional sector only if dated authority exists

features:
    breadth
    participation concentration
    cross-sectional dispersion
    leader isolation

    continuous context or veto
    not standalone entry
```

- no narrative-sector search;
- no current-survivor backfill;
- one primary breadth definition per cohort;
- evaluate incremental value on a frozen C01 or C02 event tape;
- report current-survivor breadth only as a negative/data-bias control.

Reject the context mechanism if PIT breadth/dispersion does not add stable information beyond parent-market return, volatility, and ordinary aggregate breadth.
