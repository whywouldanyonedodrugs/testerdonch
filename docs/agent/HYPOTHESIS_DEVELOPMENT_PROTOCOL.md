# Hypothesis Development Protocol

Status: active prospective method, version 2.0, effective 2026-07-19.

Machine authority: [`HYPOTHESIS_DEVELOPMENT_PROTOCOL.json`](HYPOTHESIS_DEVELOPMENT_PROTOCOL.json). Evidence limitations use the separate [`EVIDENCE_LIMITATION_TAGS.json`](EVIDENCE_LIMITATION_TAGS.json). Research routes remain exactly those in policy v1.0; this protocol does not add or rename a route.

## Purpose

A plausible hand-written translation is a testable first interpretation, not automatically the definitive confirmatory form of a qualitative mechanism. Development may characterize a mechanism in predefined folds, but it must not become unregistered same-sample rescue or leak later-fold information backward.

Protocol v2 distinguishes a family from any one translation. Closing a translation does not automatically close the economic family. A materially different, prospectively registered translation may proceed under [`FAMILY_REDEFINITION_POLICY.json`](FAMILY_REDEFINITION_POLICY.json), but it inherits the parent programme exposure and multiplicity rather than receiving a clean statistical slate. Unattended work is governed by [`RESEARCH_CAMPAIGN_PROTOCOL.json`](RESEARCH_CAMPAIGN_PROTOCOL.json) and never self-authorizes an outcome-bearing phase.

## Phases

### Phase 0 — mechanism and actor specification

State the compelled actor or balance sheet, why pressure should persist or reverse, the observable Kraken state, information availability, main null, falsification, and data-semantic limit. A generic multivariate state without a compelled actor or structural balance sheet is `mechanism_underidentified`.

### Phase 1 — measurement validation, outcome-free

Validate raw magnitude in native units and bps alongside the standardized score; event frequency and duration; year, symbol, and liquidity-cohort coverage; reference-leg authority; cost break-even magnitude; exact feature windows; and timestamp availability. Flag statistically unusual states whose raw magnitude is economically too small relative to plausible costs before any outcome work.

### Phase 2 — development-fold response characterization

This phase opens returns and always requires separate exact human approval. Use only predefined development folds and register every explored cell. Permitted purposes are continuous response surfaces, monotonicity, directional sign, horizon profile, component incrementality, and context interaction. These results are developmental, never confirmatory.

### Phase 3 — translation freeze

Freeze direction, feature form, threshold or continuous rank, horizon, payoff archetype, context, controls, costs, and multiplicity family from development evidence. Information from a later fold may never alter an earlier fold's design or record.

### Phase 4 — untouched-fold evaluation

With separate exact approval, evaluate the frozen translation on the next untouched rankable block. Apply purging and embargo where labels, events, or horizons overlap. Exposure of that block ends its untouched status; the contract cannot mutate afterward.

### Phase 5 — rolling or nested replication

Repeat development, freeze, and evaluation forward through time under separately approved runs. A future block remains hidden from all earlier design decisions.

### Phase 6 — controls, independent evidence, and deployment review

Retain the existing requirements for mechanism-relevant controls, independent or prospective validation, protected-period sealing, and deployment/risk/capacity/execution review. A research route or evidence level never implies deployment readiness.

### Phase 7 — prospective or deployment evidence

Phase 7 is separate from historical campaign approval. It requires its own authorization and may not inspect the protected period merely because Phases 2–6 were approved.

## Admission to a future full Level-3 run

Before admission, document all eight fields serialized in the machine contract: measurement semantics, mechanism-consistent event frequency, economically relevant raw magnitude, identified actor or structural mechanism, development route or explicit one-shot reason, justified horizon, defined component controls, and frozen payoff archetype.

A simple one-shot translation remains allowed only where the rule follows directly from a strong external or mechanical theory and the reason is explicit. This exception does not waive measurement validation, exact economic-run approval, untouched evaluation, or later controls/validation/deployment requirements.

## Same-sample boundary

All development cells count toward multiplicity. Failed or weak cells stay registered. A development fold may select a translation for the next untouched fold; it may not be relabelled as confirmation, and the untouched result may not be sent backward to revise thresholds, directions, horizons, components, costs, or contexts in an earlier fold.
